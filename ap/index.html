Frequently asked

Everything you need to know before we start.

How long does a typical project take?

Depends on scope. A simple landing page takes 3–5 business days. A full multi-page site usually runs 2–3 weeks. I'll give you exact timelines on our first call.

Do I need to provide content and images?

I work with whatever you have. If you've got brand assets and copy — great. If not, I'll help with structure, recommend stock imagery, and can connect you with copywriters or photographers if needed.

What about domain and hosting?

I'll help you pick and register a domain and hosting. I recommend reliable, affordable providers and handle all the technical setup so you don't have to worry about DNS or SSL.

Will the site work on mobile?

Absolutely. Every site I build is fully responsive and looks great on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Mobile-first is my default approach.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After the free support period, I offer affordable monthly plans covering updates, backups, security monitoring, and content edits — so your site stays current and secure.

What if I don't like the design?

Your satisfaction is the priority. Every package includes revisions, and during the design phase we work together to nail the style and direction before any code is written. If something's off, I'll refine it until it's right.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about working with me.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the complexity. A simple landing page can be done in 3–5 business days. A full multi-page business website typically takes 2–3 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline upfront during our discovery call.

Do I need to provide the content and images?

I can work with whatever you have! If you have brand assets and copy, great. If not, I can help with content structure, suggest stock photos, and connect you with copywriters and photographers if needed.

What about domain name and hosting?

I'll guide you through purchasing a domain name and setting up hosting. I recommend reliable, cost-effective providers and can handle the entire technical setup so you don't have to worry about DNS records or SSL certificates.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely! Every website I build is fully responsive, meaning it looks and works beautifully on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Mobile-first design is my standard approach.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! After your free support period ends, I offer affordable monthly maintenance plans that include updates, backups, security monitoring, and content changes so your site stays fresh and secure.

What if I don't like the design?

Your satisfaction is my priority. Every package includes revision rounds, and I work closely with you during the design phase to ensure we're aligned on style and direction before writing a single line of code. If you're not happy, I'll revise it until you are.
